IV. Personalization and Design File Requirements

We accept high-resolution files in **PNG, JPG, JPEG, SVG, or PDF** format. Every detail matters, so files must be clear, high-quality, and not blurry.

For the best print results, files should have a minimum of **300 DPI**. Avoid screenshots or small, pixelated images. We use a standard **CMYK color profile**.

If sending designs via WhatsApp, please send the file as a **document, not a photo**, to maintain full quality.

Print Sizes and File Limits:

  • **Tees:** Sizes range from A5 Mini (5" x 8") to A2 Max (16" x 23").
  • **Totes:** The print size is A4 Mid (8" x 11").
  • You can upload at least **three files per order**, with each file under **10MB**.

Placement and Changes:

If you have a specific design placement (front, center, corner), please let us know by adding a note during checkout or uploading a reference image. Clear file naming (e.g., "front tote image.png") is essential for accurate placement.

Once an order is placed and the design file is confirmed, **changes cannot be made after a strict 6-hour window**, as our production process begins shortly thereafter.

We cannot guarantee exact color matches due to variations in monitor settings and material absorption rates. If you are unsure about your file quality, please reach out to us for assistance via our Contact Form, WhatsApp, Call, SMS at **+234 708 005 4074**, or Email at **hello@iamxis.com.ng**.

V. Product Care and Durability

All of our prints are crisp, durable, and made to last with proper care.

Care Instructions:

  • **Tees (100% breathable cotton):** Wash inside out in cold water and hang dry. Avoid using bleach and **do not iron directly over the custom print**.
  • **Totes (100% durable cotton):** Should be **spot-cleaned gently** with mild detergent and a soft cloth. Avoid machine washing or scrubbing totes.
  • **Mugs (Glossy and Magic):** For maximum print longevity, we strongly recommend **hand washing** them with warm water and mild soap. Avoid using dishwashers for mugs.

Totes are designed for everyday carry; select totes include a lined interior with pockets for added functionality.

We offer custom made options which can be viewed in our Custom Made Edit section here - https://iamxis.com.ng/collections/custom-made/. For any special requests related to custom orders, please reach out to our support team directly.
